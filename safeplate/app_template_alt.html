METHODS — HOW EACH VALUE IS PRODUCED

01 — Menu collection

We read the real menu

For every nearby venue we locate and parse its actual menu, allergen charts, and allergy statements — dish by dish, not by cuisine label alone. What we cannot read is recorded as unread coverage.

02 — Assay

Read against a range

Each restaurant's nut-allergy risk is plotted on a published reference range against your profile. The flag combines a word, a fill pattern, a glyph, and the score's position — never colour alone.

03 — Chain of custody

Every value, traceable

Each claim carries a custody code — confirmed, signal, inferred, or estimate — and links to a numbered method note. When evidence is thin we read CAUTION and recommend a call-ahead, never a hollow "safe".

Not a medical guarantee. SafePlate estimates allergen risk from menu evidence, cuisine, and location, and reports its uncertainty plainly. Readings can be incomplete or out of date. Always confirm directly with the restaurant before ordering with a serious allergy, and carry your prescribed medication. This assay is decision support, not a clinical diagnosis.
