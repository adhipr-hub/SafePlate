Why you can trust it

Real menu evidence

Every score, explained

Each verdict shows its rationale and links to the exact source: a chart, a menu line, or a review. Nothing is asserted without proof on the desk.

Safety is asymmetric

A missed risk is far worse than a false alarm. When evidence is thin, we say so plainly and lower confidence. We never round up to a reassuring "safe."
